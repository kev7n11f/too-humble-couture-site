ORDER TRACKING

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this to monitor your package's journey.

  • Tracking emails are sent to the email address provided at checkout
  • It may take 24-48 hours for tracking information to update after shipping
  • If you haven't received tracking within 7 business days, please contact us

SHIPPING CARRIERS

We work with various carriers depending on your location and the fulfillment center:

  • USPS (United States Postal Service)
  • UPS
  • FedEx
  • DHL (for some international orders)
  • Local postal services for international delivery

DELIVERY ISSUES

Package Shows "Delivered" But Not Received:

  • Check around your property (porch, garage, mailbox)
  • Check with neighbors or building management
  • Wait 24-48 hours (packages sometimes show delivered early)
  • Contact your local carrier
  • If still missing, contact us within 7 days

Wrong Address: If you entered an incorrect address, please contact us immediately at support@toohumblecouture.com. If the order hasn't shipped yet, we may be able to update it. Once shipped, we cannot redirect packages.

Damaged Package: If your package arrives damaged, please take photos and contact us within 7 days at support@toohumblecouture.com.

P.O. BOXES & APO/FPO ADDRESSES

We can ship to P.O. Boxes and APO/FPO addresses within the United States. Please note that delivery to these addresses may take longer than standard residential delivery.

INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

Customs & Import Taxes: International orders may be subject to customs duties, import taxes, and fees imposed by the destination country. These charges are:

  • Not included in our product prices or shipping costs
  • The responsibility of the recipient
  • Collected by your local customs office upon delivery

We are not responsible for delays caused by customs processing.

FREE SHIPPING PROMOTIONS

We occasionally offer free shipping promotions. When active, these details will be displayed on our website. Free shipping typically applies to:

  • Orders over a specified minimum amount
  • Standard shipping within the United States only
  • Cannot be combined with other offers
