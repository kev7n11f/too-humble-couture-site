ORDERS & PRODUCTS

What makes Too Humble Couture different?

We're a tongue-in-cheek streetwear brand for self-aware individuals who appreciate irony. Our pieces let you broadcast your humility while looking effortlessly stylish. It's premium streetwear with a wink at the culture.

What is print-on-demand?

Print-on-demand means each item is made just for you after you order. This allows us to offer a wide variety of designs without waste. The tradeoff is slightly longer delivery times (production + shipping), but you get a freshly made product every time.

What's the quality like?

We use premium blank apparel from trusted brands. Our t-shirts are soft, pre-shrunk cotton or cotton blends. Hoodies feature heavyweight fleece with quality construction. We print using high-quality direct-to-garment (DTG) printing for vibrant, durable designs.

How do I care for my items?

To keep your THC pieces looking fresh:

  • Machine wash cold, inside out
  • Use mild detergent
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not iron directly on the print
  • Do not bleach or dry clean
How do I choose the right size?

Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements. Our pieces are designed with an oversized fit‚Äîif you prefer a more fitted look, consider sizing down. When in doubt, contact us!

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

How long will my order take?

Total delivery time = Production (2-5 business days) + Shipping (3-7 business days for US). Most US orders arrive within 7-14 business days. International orders may take longer. See our full Shipping Information.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping times vary (typically 10-30 business days). Note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the recipient's responsibility.

Where is my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. If you haven't received this email, check your spam folder. Still can't find it? Contact us at support@toohumblecouture.com with your order number.

Can I change my shipping address?

If your order hasn't entered production yet, we may be able to update the address. Contact us immediately at support@toohumblecouture.com. Once shipped, we cannot redirect packages.

RETURNS & REFUNDS

What's your return policy?

Because items are made-to-order, we can only accept returns for defective or damaged items, or if we made an error. Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order with photos of any issues. See our full Returns Policy.

My item arrived damaged‚Äîwhat do I do?

We're sorry to hear that! Please email returns@toohumblecouture.com within 7 days with your order number and photos of the damage. We'll make it right with a replacement or full refund.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. After that, if production hasn't started, we may still be able to help. Contact us immediately at support@toohumblecouture.com. Once in production, cancellation isn't possible.

Do you offer exchanges?

We don't offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, you can return the original item (if eligible) and place a new order. This ensures the fastest processing.
